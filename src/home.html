Jenkinson's Sea Life

Jenkinson’s sea life is a privately owned facility located on the boardwalk in Point Pleasant Beach, NJ. Since June of 1991, the sea life has been dedicated to educating the public on all aspects of marine life and conservation. Each exhibit is designed to promote awareness of the animals, their habitats, and conservation. We strive to provide our guests with a unique and memorable experience through creative exhibits as well as innovative educational and outreach programs.

About Sea Life

Explore the splendor of the marine kingdom at South East Asia (S.E.A) Aquarium Singapore, which is one of the largest aquariums in the world. It is an underwater wonderland featuring about 100,000 different aquatic creatures from the world's major oceans and seas. You can find a large number of exotic fishes, majestic sharks, and other sea creatures spread across eight different zones in 40 distinct habitats. The aquarium also houses rare and endangered species like the Bowmouth Guitarfish, Moray Eel, and Blue Tang. You can spot unique corals like the Bubble Coral, Sun Coral, and Brain Coral and learn more about coral reefs and their importance in the marine ecosystem.
